Welcome to Mossy Rock Network, the internet's home for content involving both rocks and moss.

We are an international community for the appreciation of mossy rocks worldwide. Established in 2021, our goal is to connect images of mossy rocks to the people who enjoy them.

A note to MRN members

November 7, 2006

Just a note for member from admin Ken. And I can't believe I have to go over this again. Lichens are NOT the same thing as mosses. We had a great season this year, but come 2007 PLEASE limit your content to moss and/or mossy rock related stuff ONLY. We don't "lichen shame" here, but come on. There's a time and place.

Cheers and see all you folks next season!
